Drink Tracking App

This is my first foray into building a mobile app. It is written with next.js and uses Vercel and Supabase. It allows users to easily track, visualize, and share their alcohol consumption. I hope that the community sharing aspect of it might help others stick to their goals. I had no prior experience with react and javascript, and built most of it with assistance from Claude. It is amazing that an amateur can quickly build things like this with LLMs!
